Before You Begin

This assessment has three short sections:

Section A explores which moral foundations you prioritize.

Section B explores how you express each foundation — there are many valid ways.

Section C explores how you hold your values overall — rigidly or flexibly, exclusively or expansively.

There are no right or wrong answers. Answer based on how you actually think and feel, not how you think you should.
